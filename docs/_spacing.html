Padding and margin utilities follow a consistent naming scheme.

Padding

p-XY (all), pt/pr/pb/pl-XY (sides), px-XY (inline), py-XY (block).

Units

  • px: 5–50 step 5 → p-25px
  • em: .25–3 step .25 → px-15em
  • %: 5–100, incl. 33,35,66 → py-33pc

Specials

p-0, px-0, … p-unset, px-unset, …

Margin

m-XY, mt/mr/mb/ml-XY, mx-XY, my-XY with same ranges. Extras: m-auto, mx-auto, m-unset.

<div class="p-1em mx-auto my-2em">Card</div>
